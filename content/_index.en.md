AvareX is a pilot's all in one electronic flight bag solution.

AvareX, written in Flutter. Runs on Linux, Windows, MacOS, iOS, Android, and Raspberry Pi.

AvareX provide FAA charts, plates, airport data, weather, air traffic over ADS-B, and lets you create and file flight plans.
